# Governance

Effective governance ensures transparency, accountability, and collaboration within the LLM Consensus System. This section outlines the governance model and decision-making processes.

## Contribution Model
- **Purpose**: Encourages community involvement while maintaining quality and consistency.
- **Practices**:
  - Define roles and responsibilities for contributors.
  - Use a code of conduct to foster a respectful environment.
  - Review and approve contributions through a structured process.

## Decision-Making Process
- **Purpose**: Ensures that decisions are made collaboratively and align with project goals.
- **Practices**:
  - Use a consensus-driven approach for major decisions.
  - Document decisions and their rationale in a decision log.
  - Hold regular meetings to discuss progress and challenges.

## Transparency and Accountability
- **Purpose**: Builds trust among stakeholders.
- **Practices**:
  - Publish meeting minutes and decision logs.
  - Provide regular updates on project status and milestones.
  - Use version control to track changes and their authors.
