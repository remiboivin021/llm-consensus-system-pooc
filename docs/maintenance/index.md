# Maintenance Tasks

Regular maintenance is essential to ensure the reliability and performance of the LLM Consensus System. This section outlines the key maintenance tasks and best practices.

## Health Checks
- **Purpose**: Monitors the system's health and identifies potential issues.
- **Practices**:
  - Implement automated health checks for critical components.
  - Schedule regular manual inspections to verify system integrity.

## Troubleshooting
- **Purpose**: Resolves issues quickly to minimize downtime.
- **Practices**:
  - Use centralized logging to identify the root cause of issues.
  - Maintain a knowledge base of common problems and their solutions.
  - Escalate unresolved issues to the appropriate team.

## Regular Updates
- **Purpose**: Keeps the system secure and up-to-date.
- **Practices**:
  - Apply security patches and software updates promptly.
  - Test updates in a staging environment before deployment.
  - Document all updates and their impact on the system.

## Backup and Recovery
- **Purpose**: Protects data and ensures business continuity.
- **Practices**:
  - Schedule regular backups of critical data.
  - Test recovery procedures to ensure they work as expected.
  - Store backups in a secure, off-site location.
